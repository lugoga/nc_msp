Q: What should I bring to a workshop? A: Please bring a laptop with R or Python installed. We’ll provide all necessary datasets and materials.

Q: Is there a registration fee? A: Most workshops are free for participants from partner institutions. Contact us for details.

Q: How long are workshops? A: Workshops typically run from 2-4 hours, depending on the topic.

Q: Do you provide certificates? A: Yes, completion certificates are provided for all workshops.

Q: Can I attend remotely? A: Some workshops are held in person at SOAF-UDSM, while others are conducted online. Details will be provided upon registration.
